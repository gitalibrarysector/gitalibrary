Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How do I become a member?

Visit our library with a valid ID and proof of address. Our staff will guide you through the membership process.

Can I book facilities online?

Yes, registered members can book study rooms and other facilities through our online booking system.
